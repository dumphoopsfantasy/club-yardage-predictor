Three themes, all based on the Dump Golf logo colors:
- **Dump** (default): Cream/gold background, deep green primary — warm light theme
- **Dump Dark**: Dark warm background, gold primary, green accents
- **Dump Green**: Deep green background, gold primary — Masters-inspired dark theme

Key palette from logo:
- Cream: HSL 48 60% 95%
- Deep Green: HSL 140 60% 28%
- Gold: HSL 48 80% 55%

Logo is always shown in the Calculator header on all themes.
